Echeveria Orion

Echeveria succulents are a genus of succulents in the Crassulaceae family. Echeveria succulents are native to many parts of Central America, specifically Mexico. Echeveria succulents are incredibly vibrant and colorful succulent plants. Echeveria succulents are treasured amongst all succulent enthusiasts for their beautiful rosette shapes.

LIGHT & SOIL

Orion enjoys substantial light for the majority of the day. This makes your Echeveria Orion ideal for any place that receives adequate light such as the windowsill or patio. It is not necessary, but to best care for your Echeveria Orion place your Echeveria Orion in a place that receives light or sun for at least 50% of the day. Your Echeveria Orion will grow best in a well-draining soil. Your Echeveria Orion will already come potted and rooted in a well-draining soil, but if you plan to transplant your Echeveria Orion it is best to utilize a well-draining potting mix.

SUCCULENT CARE

Your Echeveria Orion enjoys a dry and arid environment. For this reason, it is best to only water your Echeveria Orion when the soil is completely dry. Feel the top of the soil or remove your Echeveria Orion from its pot and feel the soil. If there is any moisture at all in the soil do not water. When watering your Echeveria Orion completely drench the soil to a point that the soil is completely moist. Your Echeveria Orion cannot survive in temperatures below 30 degrees Fahrenheit.
